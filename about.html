About Utsavam Food and Hospitality

Utsavam Food and Hospitality Private Limited

Utsavam is built on a decade of work under the Fresh & Natural's legacy since 2014, delivering high-quality food and beverage services across India with consistency, punctuality, fresh ingredients, and multi-cuisine expertise.

The brand brings together traditional recipes, innovative menus, trained chefs, and disciplined operations to create memorable experiences for corporate dining, industrial catering, weddings, social events, outdoor catering, Pantry Consumables, Hot Beverages and retail food services.

Our Focus & Priority

Health, Safety, Environment and Quality at every step.

Utsavam places HSEQ at the core of operations, protecting people, clients, and communities through compliance, continuous improvement, and collaborative standards.

Health

Protecting physical and mental well-being through proactive awareness and healthy work practices.

Safety

Strict protocols, regular training, and a shared responsibility for safe on-site and off-site work.

Environment

Sustainable practices that reduce impact and respect environmental regulations.

Quality

Consistent outcomes shaped by best practices, customer feedback, and continuous improvement.

Sustainability

Responsible hospitality with care for tomorrow.

At Utsavam, sustainability is part of everyday operations. We work toward mindful sourcing, efficient kitchen planning, reduced food waste, and responsible service practices that support clients, communities, and the environment.

01

Mindful sourcing

Preference for fresh, seasonal ingredients and vendor partnerships that support quality, traceability, and reliability.

02

Waste reduction

Careful menu planning, portion control, and production discipline help reduce avoidable food waste.

03

Responsible service

Thoughtful packaging choices, cleaner work practices, and efficient logistics keep service dependable while reducing impact.
